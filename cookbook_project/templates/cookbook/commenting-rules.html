Commenting rules on CookBook

The general rule for commenting is: be nice. Remember that if you break the rules, your comments may be deleted, and your account deleted as well if we judge it necessary. Here are the guidelines for commenting.

  • Do not post racist, sexist, homophobic, or otherwise bigoted comments.
  • Do not post threats or encourage self-harm.
  • Do not post or link to anything pornographic, or illegal (including illegal file sharing).
  • Do not spam: no excessive posting, repeated self-promotion, or paid referral links.
  • Do not insult other users.
  • If you want to criticise a recipe or suggest changes, be constructive: explain why you think that the recipe is imperfect and give your suggestions.
