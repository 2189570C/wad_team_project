Frequently Asked Questions

How do I write a recipe?

You can find guidelines for writing recipes here.

There is a bug on the website, what do I do?

If you find a bug on our website, please contact Peter Maitland at 2200049m@student.gla.ac.uk, explaining the bug, what you were doing when it happened, and a screenshot if possible. We will keep you up to date on how we are dealing with the bug.

I want to report a user, who can I contact?

You should only report users that have broken the commenting rules. If you want to report a user, you can write an email to Hope Doolan at xxxxxxxD@student.gla.ac.uk, state your username, the username of the person you want to report and why you want to report him with links to pages where you think the user may have broken some rules.

I have a suggestion for the website, who can I contact to pitch my idea?

If you have an idea that you want for improving CookBook that you want to share with us, contact Iain Cattermole at 2189570c@student.gla.ac.uk. We may use your idea and make the site even better!

I would like to work with/for CookBook, who can I contact?

If you want to work for us or in partnership with us, please contact Lukas Backis at 2145673B@student.gla.ac.uk. {% endblock %}
